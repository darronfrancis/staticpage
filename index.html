ABOUT US

Archusa Memorial Gardens was established in 1978 by Rev. James M. Seale, who saw a need for a perpetual care cemetery for the community. It was created to be a place for remembering and memorializing those who have departed this life. Compassionately caring for the human remains entrusted to our keeping with dignity and respect is our mission.

Conveniently located along Highway 145, just north of Quitman, MS, our service area includes a 40-mile radius which easily serves families from Clarke, Lauderdale, and Wayne Counties.

We Exist To

...offer a peaceful place to remember those who have departed this life
...handle with dignity and respect those who have been entrusted to our care
...compassionately care for families as we serve them by assisting them in memorialization of their loved ones
